# 🧾 **Practice Sheet – GST (HSN + IGST vs CGST/SGST)**

## 🎯 **Objective**

Students will:

* Create items with **HSN codes**
* Generate **2 invoices**
* Understand the difference between:

  * **Intrastate (CGST + SGST)**
  * **Interstate (IGST)**

---

## 🏢 **Business Setup**

* **Company Name:** `Your Company Name`
* **Business Location:** Karnataka (KA)

---

## 📦 **Step 1: Create Items (with HSN Code)**

Create the following items:

### **Item 1**

* Name: Wireless Keyboard
* Selling Price: ₹1,000
* GST Rate: 18%
* **HSN Code:** 847160

### **Item 2**

* Name: USB Cable
* Selling Price: ₹200
* GST Rate: 12%
* **HSN Code:** 854442

---

## 👤 **Step 2: Create Customers**

### **Customer 1 (Same State – Intrastate)**

* Name: Bangalore Retailers
* State: Karnataka

### **Customer 2 (Different State – Interstate)**

* Name: Mumbai Electronics
* State: Maharashtra

---

## 🧾 **Step 3: Create Invoices**

---

### ✅ **Invoice 1 – Intrastate (Same State)**

* Customer: Bangalore Retailers
* Place of Supply: Karnataka
* Items:

  * Wireless Keyboard – Qty: 2
  * USB Cable – Qty: 3

👉 **Expected GST:**

* CGST (9%)
* SGST (9%)

---

### ✅ **Invoice 2 – Interstate (Different State)**

* Customer: Mumbai Electronics
* Place of Supply: Maharashtra
* Items:

  * Wireless Keyboard – Qty: 2
  * USB Cable – Qty: 3

👉 **Expected GST:**

* IGST (18%)

---

## 🔍 **Step 4: Verification Tasks**

Students must verify:

1. HSN codes are correctly shown in both invoices
2. GST applied correctly:

   * Intrastate → CGST + SGST
   * Interstate → IGST
3. Tax amounts are calculated correctly
4. Total invoice values match expectations

---

## 🧠 **Questions for Students**

1. Why is IGST applied in the second invoice?
2. Why are CGST and SGST applied in the first invoice?
3. What happens if Place of Supply is entered incorrectly?
4. Where is HSN code visible in the invoice?
